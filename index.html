Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your automated cleaning system

Will this void my solar panel warranty?
▼
No, absolutely not. Our system uses a non-invasive installation method with zero drilling or screwing into your solar panels. We connect directly to your existing water spigot and mount the distribution network alongside your panels. This safe installation method maintains your full manufacturer warranty with your solar provider.
How is it installed? How long does it take?
▼
Installation is straightforward and takes 2-3 hours for most systems. We connect the system to your existing outdoor water spigot, mount the pure water filter and smart timer in a weatherproof location, and distribute the network along your roof edges. No plumbing or electrical experience is required. Our licensed technicians handle everything, and the system is ready to start cleaning automatically once we finish.
Is there maintenance required?
▼
Minimal. The system requires a filter replacement once per year, which you can easily do yourself for approximately $40. Simply remove the old filter cartridge and insert the new one—no tools or expertise needed.

Alternatively, we offer an annual maintenance package where our team handles the filter replacement and performs a complete system inspection to ensure optimal performance. Contact us for pricing and details on our maintenance packages.
Will it work in Las Vegas heat and hard water?
▼
Yes, designed for it. Our system is heat-rated to 200°F+, which far exceeds Las Vegas summer temperatures. The pure water filtration system eliminates total dissolved solids (TDS) and mineral deposits, meaning your panels stay spotless regardless of water hardness. The system thrives in our desert climate—that's exactly why we engineered it.
What happens if something breaks?
▼
You're covered by our lifetime warranty, which includes both parts and labor. If any component fails for any reason, we'll repair or replace it at no cost. We stand behind our product completely. Plus, we offer 24/7 support—if you experience any issues or questions, call us at 702-482-9025 and we'll get it resolved quickly.
